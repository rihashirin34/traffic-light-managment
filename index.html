Technical Implementation

1. The Problem

Traditional traffic lights use fixed timers, leading to inefficient traffic flow.

2. Our Solution

Our system uses IR sensors to count vehicles. The CPU calculates green signal time based on density.

Time = max(10, Car_Count * 3) seconds

3. Emergency Protocol

When an emergency vehicle is detected, the current light terminates and the emergency lane turns Green immediately.
